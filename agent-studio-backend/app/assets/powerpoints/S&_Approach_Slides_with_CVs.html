Slide 6 — Step 1: Discover & Diagnose

Establish baseline understanding and identify priority improvement areas

Step 1: Discover & Diagnose

Key Activities
1.1 Stakeholder Alignment & Interviews
Conduct executive sponsor interviews to confirm vision and success criteria
Facilitate cross-functional working sessions with key process owners
1.2 Current State Assessment
Map existing processes, systems, and data flows across functions
Benchmark performance against industry standards and best practices
1.3 Gap Analysis & Prioritization
Identify capability gaps and root causes of performance issues
Develop prioritized opportunity matrix based on value and feasibility
Deliverables
1Stakeholder Interview Synthesis Report
2Current State Process Maps & Pain Points
3Benchmarking Analysis & Scorecard
4Prioritized Opportunity Roadmap

Slide 7 — Step 2: Design & Develop

Architect target state solutions and build implementation roadmap

Step 2: Design & Develop

Key Activities
2.1 Solution Architecture & Blueprint
Define target operating model with roles, responsibilities, and governance
Design technology architecture and integration requirements
2.2 Process Redesign & Optimization
Develop future-state process flows with automation opportunities
Define KPIs and measurement framework for continuous improvement
2.3 Change Roadmap & Governance
Build phased implementation plan with dependencies and milestones
Establish governance structure and decision-making protocols
Deliverables
1Target Operating Model Blueprint
2Future-State Process Documentation
3Technology Architecture & Requirements
4Implementation Roadmap & Governance Charter

Slide 8 — Step 3: Deploy & Deliver

Execute implementation with structured pilots and enterprise rollout

Step 3: Deploy & Deliver

Key Activities
3.1 Pilot Implementation & Testing
Execute controlled pilot with selected business unit or geography
Conduct user acceptance testing and iterate based on feedback
3.2 Training & Capability Building
Develop training curriculum and enablement materials
Deliver train-the-trainer sessions and certification programs
3.3 Full Rollout & Transition
Execute enterprise-wide deployment with hypercare support
Transition to business-as-usual with continuous improvement cadence
Deliverables
1Pilot Results & Lessons Learned Report
2Training Materials & Certification Framework
3Deployed Solution with User Adoption Metrics
4BAU Transition Plan & Support Model
